Rice

This is a recipe for rice.

  • Jasmine rice
  • Water
  • Rice cooker
  1. Add any number of cups of rice to the rice cooker pot.
  2. Wash the rice with cold water 2-3 times to remove excess starch.
  3. Place your fingertip on top of the rice and add water until it reaches your first knuckle.
  4. Put the pot in the rice cooker and cook the rice.
